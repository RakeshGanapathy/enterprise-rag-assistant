# Incident Response Runbook

## Severity Levels

Sev-1 incidents are critical production outages or security events that affect most customers or expose sensitive data. Sev-2 incidents affect a limited customer segment or have a known workaround.

## Sev-1 Process

For a Sev-1 incident, the on-call engineer must acknowledge the alert within 5 minutes, open an incident channel, assign an incident commander, and post status updates every 15 minutes until resolution.

## Postmortem

A postmortem must be completed within 3 business days for Sev-1 and Sev-2 incidents. The postmortem should include timeline, root cause, customer impact, corrective actions, and owners.
