# Product FAQ

## Login Issues

Most login issues are caused by expired sessions, incorrect identity provider configuration, or blocked third-party cookies. Support should first ask the customer to confirm their identity provider settings.

## Data Export

Workspace administrators can export audit logs and usage reports from the admin console. Exports are generated as CSV files and remain available for 7 days.

## Support Priority

Enterprise customers receive priority support for production issues. Security-related support tickets should be escalated to the security operations queue.
