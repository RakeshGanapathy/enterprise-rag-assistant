# HR Policy Handbook

## Paid Time Off

Full-time employees receive 20 paid time off days per calendar year. Employees may carry over up to 5 unused PTO days into the next calendar year. Any PTO above the 5-day carryover limit expires on December 31.

## Sick Leave

Employees receive 10 sick leave days per calendar year. Sick leave does not require advance notice, but employees should notify their manager as soon as practical.

## Remote Work

Employees may work remotely up to 3 days per week with manager approval. Teams handling regulated customer data must follow the secure workspace policy.
